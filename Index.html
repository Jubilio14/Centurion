Our Philosophy

Disciplined capital.
Profitable growth.

We are not venture capital. We do not chase unicorns. We back real businesses run by serious founders who need growth capital to move faster.
01
Profitability First
Every investment must generate cash returns within 12 months. Not paper gains — real money back. This is non-negotiable and overrides all other criteria.
02
Partner, Not Lender
We invest alongside you with flexible structures — revenue share, profit share, convertible notes, or hybrid models. We succeed when you succeed.
03
Speed & Simplicity
No 40-page pitch decks. No 6-month processes. We make decisions fast because founders cannot afford to wait. Tell us the numbers, show us the plan.
04
Execution Over Ideas
A warung with proven unit economics is more investable than a brilliant app with no revenue. We care about what exists today, not what might exist someday.
05
Operator Involvement
We do not disappear after writing the cheque. We provide operational guidance, financial discipline, and strategic support to protect our shared investment.
06
Sector Agnostic
From food stalls to digital businesses, trading companies to service firms. If the unit economics work and the founder is serious, we are interested.
